# Hospital Emergency Room Analytic
## Project Objective
To develop an interactive Power BI dashboard for analyzing Emergency Room  patient data, tracking operational KPIs such as patient volume, wait time, satisfaction score, admissions, and referrals. The objective is to leverage data analytics and DAX-driven insights to optimize resource allocation, improve service efficiency, and support data-driven healthcare decision-making.

## Conclusion
- The Healthcare Performance & Patient Metrics Analytics Dashboard analyzed 9216 Emergency Room patient visits to evaluate hospital operational efficiency, patient experience, and resource utilization. The analysis revealed a nearly equal distribution between admitted and non-admitted patients, indicating balanced case severity within the Emergency Department. An average patient wait time of approximately 35 minutes and an average satisfaction score of 4.99/10 highlighted opportunities to improve service quality and patient experience. Referral analysis showed that General Practice and Orthopedics handled the highest referral volumes, emphasizing the need for optimized resource allocation in these departments. Patient demographic analysis demonstrated a diverse patient population with a balanced gender distribution, while operational trends indicated consistent patient demand throughout the study period.
